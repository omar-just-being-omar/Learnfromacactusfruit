NASA Space Apps Challenge

We created a web application that teaches students about the universe using efficient and fun ways.

The Problem

Many students find learning about the universe difficult or boring — traditional methods fail to engage them.

Our Solution

Our web app uses interactive tools, visuals, quizzes, and short simulations to teach complex space concepts quickly and enjoyably.

Impact

Make space science easier to understand and inspire the next generation of explorers.
